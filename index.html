👥 Community Guidelines

🕊️ Our Mission

SafeVoice provides a secure, anonymous platform for students to share their experiences with mental health, academic pressure, and other challenges. We believe every voice matters.

🛡️ How We Protect You

  • Complete anonymity - no personal data collected
  • End-to-end encryption for all posts
  • No IP address tracking
  • Optional wallet feature (not required)
  • Community-driven moderation

❤️ Community Guidelines

  • Be kind and supportive
  • Respect others' experiences
  • No hate speech or bullying
  • Report harmful content
  • Seek help if you need it

💡 Get Involved

Help us build a supportive community by sharing your story, offering support through reactions, and spreading awareness about mental health.
